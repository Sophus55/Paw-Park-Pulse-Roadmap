1. Overview

Paw Park Pulse ("Paw Park Pulse", "we", "us", or "the App") helps dog owners discover dog parks, check in, and share community input. This Privacy Policy explains what personal data we collect, how we use it, and your choices. By using the App you agree to this Policy.

3.2 Profile (Optional Fields You Provide)

3.8 Minimal Operational Analytics (Anonymous / Opt-Out)

We log a very limited internal event when a user initiates a subscription attempt so we can understand paywall performance and reliability.

Event fields:

  • action (e.g., "subscription_attempt")
  • plan (single | family)
  • source (UI context, e.g., paywall_cta, plan_change)
  • timestamp
  • user_id (UID or "anonymous" if not signed in at that moment)

No advertising identifiers, no precise location, and no payment card data are included. You can disable this logging at any time via Settings > Account > Anonymous Analytics toggle. When disabled, no new events are written (existing historical events are not retroactively removed but can be deleted upon verified request, subject to legal limits).

Not Collected

4. How We Use Your Data

We use data to:

  • Provide and maintain core functionality (authentication, park search, check‚Äëins)
  • Display and attribute your submitted content
  • Process subscriptions and manage entitlements
  • Respond to support requests
  • Improve app performance and fix bugs
  • Understand subscription conversion (minimal anonymous analytics, opt-out available)

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data.

We share data only in these limited cases:

  • Firebase / Google Cloud: Backend infrastructure (authentication, database, storage). Google processes data per their Firebase Privacy terms.
  • Cloudinary: Image hosting service for park photos and profile pictures. Images are stored on Cloudinary's servers per their privacy policy.
  • Apple: Subscription billing is handled by Apple via the App Store. We do not see your payment details.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose data if required by law, court order, or to protect safety and rights.

6. Data Retention & Deletion

Account Deletion

You can delete your account at any time via Settings > Account > Delete Account. Upon deletion:

  • Your profile data and authentication records are deleted within 24 hours
  • Your uploaded photos are removed from Cloudinary
  • Park suggestions and reviews you submitted are anonymized (they remain visible but are no longer tied to your identity)
  • Check-ins are removed

Data Retention

We retain data as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. After account deletion, some anonymized or aggregated data may be retained for legal, security, or operational purposes.

7. Security

We use industry-standard security measures including:

  • Firebase Authentication for secure login
  • Firestore Security Rules to protect user data
  • HTTPS encryption for data in transit
  • Access controls limiting who can view or modify data

Note: There is no end-to-end encryption for user content. Standard platform security (Firebase, Cloudinary) applies. No system is 100% secure‚Äîuse the app at your own risk.

8. Children's Privacy

Paw Park Pulse is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it.

9. Your Rights & Choices

Access & Correction

You can view and edit your profile data in Settings > Account.

Location Permissions

You can disable location access at any time via your device settings (Settings > Paw Park Pulse > Location). Disabling location will limit park discovery features.

Analytics Opt-Out

You can disable anonymous subscription analytics via Settings > Account > Anonymous Analytics.

Account Deletion

Delete your account via Settings > Account > Delete Account.

Data Portability

You can request a copy of your data by contacting us at the email below.

To exercise these rights, contact us at support@pawparkpulse.com.

13. European Privacy Rights (GDPR)

  • Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to restrict processing
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to object to processing

To exercise these rights, contact us at support@pawparkpulse.com. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority in your country.
